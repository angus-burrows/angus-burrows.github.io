What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

mindfulness is about setting an intention for every single thing you do, be it breathing or gardening. self awareness is the act of listening with intention, where you are submitting yourself to full immersion of the thing you are interacting to.
What was your experience of body scanning? How did you feel?

In my yoga practice, body scanning is done at the end of a class to give yourself time to think and reflect on how your body is feeling. i enjoy this alot and it makes me feel very relaxed.
What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me"

that the emotions we harbour on a daily basis are going to be different for the days to come. the only true way to be emotional intelligent is to work with yourself rather than only take control of your emotions
