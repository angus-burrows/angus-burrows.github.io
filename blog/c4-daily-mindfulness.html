Angus Burrows



Daily mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I learnt that being present and listening with intention brings forward a discovery of new information only found when having a conversation. The other person felt rewarded and opened to the idea that being mindful when listening and talking was part of being an excellent communicator.
Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I intend to focus on the walking meditation to join my pre-exsiting practice. I had chosen this as my awareness is currently very strong around mindfulness in conversation, so I want to ensure I remember to keep a inward reflective practice ongoing, to sustain and re-energise myself when I am feeling low.
